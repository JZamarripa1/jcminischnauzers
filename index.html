Pricing

Our puppies pricing ranges from $1500 to $2000 depending on the color. Pricing includes full AKC registration.

Purchasing process

  1. Contact (email or text/call) JC MS to indentify the puppy you intend to buy.
  2. Set up a quick call or video chat with Joe to indentify the right puppy for you.
  3. A $300 refundable deposit will be requested to reserve puppie. (Our puppies are in high demand).
  4. Schedule pick-up of your puppie. (We are flexible on dates and times, and can meet halfway depending on location).
  5. Remaining balance due at pick-up. (Cash or Zelle only).
